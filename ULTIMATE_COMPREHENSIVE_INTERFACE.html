Manipulative vs Honest Language

Manipulative Version

"Studies show this approach is proven effective and must be implemented immediately."

  • No studies cited
  • No proof provided
  • "Must" without authority
  • "Immediately" without reason
Honest Version

"Smith et al. (2023) found this approach increased efficiency by 34% (p < 0.01). We recommend implementation within 6 months."

  • Specific study cited
  • Exact percentage given
  • "Recommend" not "must"
  • Reasonable timeline
